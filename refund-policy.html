Refund Policy

Last updated:

1. Refund Eligibility

CountDownly offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for all premium subscription purchases. If you are not satisfied with our Service, you may request a full refund within 30 days of your initial purchase.

2. Refund Period

  • Refund requests must be made within 30 days of the original purchase date
  • The 30-day period begins on the date of your first subscription payment
  • Refund requests made after 30 days will not be honored, except as required by law

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  1. Contact us at support@countdownly.com
  2. Include your account email address and order information
  3. State the reason for your refund request
  4. Wait for our confirmation email (typically within 24-48 hours)

4. Refund Processing

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days after approval
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase
  • If the original payment method is no longer available, we will contact you for an alternative method
  • You will receive email confirmation once the refund has been processed

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Subscription renewals after the initial 30-day period
  • Partial subscription periods (no prorated refunds)
  • Refund requests made more than 30 days after purchase
  • Accounts terminated for violation of our Terms of Service

6. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period. You will continue to have access to premium features until the end of the paid period.

Note: Canceling your subscription does not automatically trigger a refund. If you wish to cancel and receive a refund, you must request the refund within the 30-day guarantee period.

7. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider instead of contacting us first, we reserve the right to terminate your account immediately. We encourage you to contact us directly to resolve any billing issues.

8. Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. Continued use of our Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

9. Special Circumstances

In exceptional circumstances, we may consider refund requests beyond the 30-day period on a case-by-case basis. Such requests should be submitted in writing with detailed explanations.

10. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@countdownly.com
Subject: Refund Request
Website: countdownly.com

We typically respond to refund requests within 24-48 hours during business days.
